What age groups does Gateway Elite Club offer?

Our Club teams are open to athletes in U-8, U-10, U-12, U-14, U-16, and U-19. Club divisions are based on birth year, please see our club page for more information about which age your athlete falls into. Our seasonal programs (League, Mini Sticks, and Prep Club) are offered based on grade and are typically blocked with two grades together.

What age groups do you offer other programs for?

Gateway Leagues and Prep Club are open to athletes in 1st through 12th grade. Mini Sticks is open to Pre-Kindergarten Junior Kindergarten, and Kindergarten athletes. Gateway camps are open to athletes rising into K through 12th grade.
